WHO WE ARE

Bluebird Soccer Academy is a Volunteer-based, charitable sports team established by refugee youth on August 2018 in Uganda. Bluebird Soccer Academy is committed to its mission of restoring hope among the needy children in Sub-Saharan Africa by fully supporting their soccer talents and skills while providing them with access to quality and better education. The blues family also have a rescue program which majorly focuses on identifying total Vulnerable unaccompanied orphans in the team and the community in order to help them get placed in good homes.

VISION

“To enrich lives of young children who will transform to be socially responsible leaders in the communities they live”.

MISSION.

We seek to provide children with access to quality education as they acquire strong mentorship programs that will help them improve on their life styles, soccer talent and skills while creating a self-reliant community.

MOTTO

“Every child can be a champion if helped to achieve their dreams”
