OUR CORE VALUES

✓ Diversity

↣ We appreciate and believe that every person is exceptional and has a strong capacity to assist blue bird soccer academy in achieving its mission of rescuing and supporting children’s dreams regardless of their race, ethnicity, religious beliefs, gender, social-economic status and nationality. We work with everyone ready to keep our dream alive and stronger.

✓ Integrity.

↣ We are deeply committed to total transparency, honesty and establishing a strong trust and working relationship with all our stakeholders. Maintainaning clear track records of all events is all we care for with utmost faith.

✓ Dedication.

↣ Our team offers all their best efforts to support our children by making sure they stay happy and connected to their dreams. We provide one-on-one mentorship care to every child so that each of them benefits equally.

✓ Community responsibility.

↣ We commit to fulfilling our promises to the communities we serve by giving in all we can to raise and nurture kids via our sports program. The community embraces and cheers work done by the academy which obliges us to invest more in the kids we support.

✓ Boldness.

↣ We are confident in fighting against child poverty, child illiteracy, child discrimination and child neglect in the communities we serve. Bluebird soccer academy is a home to a lot of children living in desperate conditions with no or little support towards their dream achievement. Every child at our academy is a champion.

✓ Accountability.

↣ We value the ability of our staff to honor commitments to each other and our beneficiaries. At bluebird soccer academy, everyone is held responsible and answerable to all matters arising at the premises.

✓ Servant leadership.

↣ We are committed to building strong communities through various sports programs by offering service with humility and compassion through empowering kids with strong soccer skills and mentorships. We are profoundly aware of the suffering young children in the sub-Saharan Africa undergo which gives us the opportunity to alleviate the misery promptly.

OUR FOCUS.
Bluebird Soccer Academy focuses on children’s access to quality education, soccer talent development,
character development with much exposure to innovations and creativity.
We also concentrate on children’s access to necessary basic necessities, appropriate welfare
and advocating for their rights to maintain their positive state of balance.
